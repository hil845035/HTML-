About Sade Hillard

Sade Hillard grew up in Brooklyn, MD and attended Maryland Academy of Technology Health Sciences (MATHS) Charter High School. In high school, Sade took interest in music, and electrical engineering. She also soared scholastically and became a member of The National Society of High School Scholars. She earned the title Valedictorian of Class 2012 through persistence in all her subjects. In her free time, Sade enjoys reading tech magazines, listening to music and spending time with family and friends. After high school, she studied General Engineering at the Community College of Baltimore County in Essex, MD. Sade took a semester off to study IT on her own at N-Power Tech Fundamentals program. After completing the program and earning a CompTIA A+ certification, she was introduced to Year Up by a friend. She decided Year Up would be a valuable tool to embark on her professional development journey to become a Cyber Security Analyst. Sade was accepted to the Year Up program in January 2018 into the Cyber Security track. She plans to gain soft skills and use her experience from IT and cyber security to help companies protect and secure information that allow them to serve clients. She plans to earn her bachelor's degree and master's degree in Cyber Security and Assurance and later become a Cyber Security Analyst.
